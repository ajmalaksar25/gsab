## LinkedIn
> I gave this app **no database**.
>
> The backend is a Google Sheet.
>
> I edit one cell → the app updates about a second later. No webhooks, no change stream —
> one `watch()` call.
>
> It's polling, not push (~1s), so it's honest about the delay. Which is exactly honest
> enough for **dashboards, internal tools, and small-team apps** where a Sheet is already
> the source of truth.
>
> We overbuild tiny tools. Sometimes a spreadsheet is enough — and now it can feel live.
>
