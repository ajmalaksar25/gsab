- The hook is the **contradiction**: "no database" → reveal the Sheet → it updates. Don't
  soften it; the tension is the share-trigger ("the kind of wrong that's useful").

  Do not imply push/websockets.
- Captions must read **muted**; the VO reinforces, the captions carry.
